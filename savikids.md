# SaviKids

An edtech I am co-founding that adapts learning to neurodivergent children.
Product: https://savikids.com

## The problem
One-size-fits-all teaching material fails children with dyslexia, ADHD or anxiety. They need content shaped to how they actually focus and learn, which does not scale with human authoring alone.

## What it does
SaviKids turns a lesson into a personalised, AI-generated story tailored to each child's needs, with tutoring and gamification to keep them engaged. The same learning objective is re-expressed in the format that works for that child.

## How it is built
LLM generation produces and adapts the stories, parameterised by each child's profile, wrapped in a full-stack product (a web and app experience) with the tutoring and gamification logic around it. Generation is constrained and reviewed so the content stays age-appropriate and on-objective.

## Stack
LLM content generation, full-stack product (web and mobile), profile-driven personalisation.

## Why it matters
The goal is to make learning genuinely accessible and engaging for neurodivergent kids, turning a hard authoring problem into something AI can scale responsibly.

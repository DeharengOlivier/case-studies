# Aurelona

My AI consulting and delivery practice (freelance).

## What it is
Aurelona is the practice I founded to help small companies and a startup adopt AI in a way that actually sticks. It is consulting plus engineering in one, from first conversation to a deployed, used system.

## How I work
I run the full arc myself. I scope the business problem and frame the value, design the solution, then build and ship it, and finally train the team so adoption outlives the engagement. Deliverables have ranged from full-stack AI web applications to agentic workflow automations.

## Stack
Full-stack development, LLM applications and agents, workflow automation, with the surrounding work of scoping, delivery and enablement.

## Why it matters
Most AI projects fail at adoption, not at the model. Aurelona exists to close that gap, by owning both the engineering and the change management rather than handing over a prototype and leaving.
